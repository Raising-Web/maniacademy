About us

Laishram Mani Memorial
English Academy

Founded in loving memory of Late Shri Laishram Mani Singh, former Deputy Director of CADA and the Department of Horticulture, Government of Manipur, Laishram Mani Memorial English Academy stands as a beacon of learning and dedication at Kongba Dhobi Lampak, Imphal East.

What began as Christ Reign School — a humble dream of a visionary who believed in the power of education to transform lives — has now blossomed into one of the most respected educational institutions along the Kongba-Yairipok Road. For more than three decades, the Academy has been nurturing young minds with knowledge, discipline, and moral strength.

Over the years, our students have carried the school's spirit of excellence to the highest levels of learning. Some of our proud alumni are now pursuing their dreams in India's top institutions — Wahengbam Naothoi, pursuing a Ph.D. at IIT Bombay, and Mutum Rajesh, completing his M.Tech. at IISc Bangalore.

Their journeys stand as shining examples of what dedicated guidance, hard work, and strong values can achieve. They are not only the pride of our Academy but also living reflections of our founder's vision — to enlighten generations through education and purpose.

At Laishram Mani Memorial English Academy, we continue to walk on this path — inspiring, guiding, and nurturing every learner to reach the height of their potential, just as our founder dreamed.

Our Mission

To cultivate knowledge, character, and discipline, shaping responsible and confident citizens of tomorrow.
